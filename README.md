# Amazon Customer Review Sentiment Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard designed to analyze Amazon customer reviews and uncover actionable insights from customer feedback. The dashboard explores sentiment distribution, review activity trends, geographic review patterns, and country-level sentiment performance to support data-driven decision-making.

By transforming customer reviews into visual insights, this analysis helps identify customer satisfaction trends, monitor review behavior over time, and highlight markets that may require strategic attention.

## Business Objectives

This project answers the following business questions:

1. What is the overall sentiment distribution of customer reviews?
2. How does customer review activity change over time?
3. Which countries generate the highest volume of customer feedback?
4. How does sentiment vary across different countries?
5. What proportion of customer reviews are positive, neutral, and negative?

---

## Dashboard Components

### 1. Sentiment Overview
Provides a high-level view of customer sentiment by categorizing reviews as Positive, Neutral, or Negative.

### 2. Sentiment Share (%)
Shows the percentage distribution of customer sentiment to better understand overall customer perception.

### 3. Customer Review Trend
Tracks review volume over time to identify seasonal patterns and changes in customer engagement.

### 4. Reviews by Country
Highlights the geographic distribution of customer reviews and identifies the most active markets.

### 5. Sentiment Distribution by Country
Compares sentiment performance across countries to uncover market-specific customer experience trends.

---

## Key Findings

- Negative reviews account for 68.15% of total customer feedback.
- Positive reviews represent 27.64% of all reviews.
- Neutral reviews account for 4.20% of customer feedback.
- Customer review activity peaks during December, indicating increased engagement during the holiday shopping season.
- The United States and United Kingdom generate the highest volume of customer reviews.
- Negative sentiment dominates across major review-generating markets, highlighting opportunities for customer experience improvement.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Investigate common themes driving negative customer sentiment.
- Prioritize customer experience improvements in high-volume markets such as the United States and United Kingdom.
- Monitor customer feedback trends during peak shopping periods.
- Establish ongoing sentiment monitoring to identify emerging customer concerns and improve retention.
