# Amazon Customer Review Sentiment Analysis

## Project Overview

This Tableau dashboard analyzes Amazon customer reviews to identify sentiment patterns, review trends, and geographic review distribution.

## Business Questions

1. What is the overall sentiment distribution of customer reviews?
2. How does review volume change over time?
3. Which countries generate the most reviews?
4. How does sentiment vary across countries?
5. What percentage of reviews belong to each sentiment category?

## Dashboard Features

- Sentiment Overview
- Sentiment Share (%)
- Customer Review Trend
- Reviews by Country
- Sentiment Distribution by Country

## Key Insights

- Negative reviews account for 68.15% of all reviews.
- Positive reviews represent 27.64%.
- Review activity peaks during December.
- The United States and United Kingdom generate the highest review volume.
- Negative sentiment dominates across major markets.
